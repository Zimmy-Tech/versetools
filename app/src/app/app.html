Welcome to VerseTools!

This is "Tier 0" of the app. We are working hard on new features and refining some missing formulas and info:

Right now, I am working on cracking the cross-section radar math. Some of the values are estimated, some are pulled from the game directly. EM and IR Signatures are more reliable than CS, and I've made solid progress through an awful lot of testing. They will likely refine and get better shortly. Some are within 1% of in-game displayed values (while the ship is static).

It would be easy to just copy the values from in-game, but in the long term, that's a less reliable and much more labor intensive practice than understanding how they are calculated by the game, and having the formula and the data be durable through patches.

Some Missions payouts are still a work in progress as well, as I finish evaluating the data.

FPS Armor Quality bonuses are currently under investigation so that we can provide a better understanding of what the quality bonuses actually do for your character in-game.

Please use the SUBMIT DATA feature if you'd like to contribute information or feedback. I encourage community participation and welcome feature requests and feedback.

I have recently added Mining Locations, Default Loadout Item Finder, Bulk Equip and Stored Loadout DPS comparisons. I am constantly updating the app, so stay tuned.

This app is in no way associated with CIG/RSI. It is a vision I've had for roughly a decade at this point.

A lot of the data and calculations you see on the site are the result of being a heavily involved player of the game for that time. I hope you find that the quality of the data and the many hours of testing that went in to get some of them is valuable.

My game data is sourced from an upcoming project that you will hear about soon. I think you will enjoy his work as well.

Feel free to pop in the Discord, the link is at the top. I'd love to hear your thoughts on the tool and ways you think it could be better or more accurate.
